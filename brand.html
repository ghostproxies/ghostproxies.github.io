Logo

The GhostProxies logo is a minimalist ghost shape that resembles a modular computer chip.

It must always be centered and textless in either black-on-white or white-on-black.

The 3 lines each represent a bar within a benchmarking chart to convey impactful computational efficiency gains.

The connecting arc line represents an embedded computing conduit that transforms results from inefficiency to peak efficiency.

Identity

GhostProxies and William Stafford Parsons are intertwined deeply and personified with the William the Wraith mascot illustration as seen on the GhostProxies homepage.

The "ghost" aspect conveys that GhostProxies provides computational randomness innovation as an "eternal" and "invisible" component of critical computing infrastructure.

The "proxies" aspect conveys that GhostProxies provides modular, universal software "conduits" that permanently replace less-efficient components for substantial efficiency gains.

The essence of the bold GhostProxies brand presence is minimalist, non-redundant brevity combined with disruptive, dominant technical results that speak for themselves.
